NEON DASH

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Welcome to Neon Dash. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information and data security when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect and Share

Neon Dash does not require account registration via email or social media. However, we collect and share the following data:

  • User-Provided Information: We collect the Username (Nickname) you choose for the Global Leaderboard. This name is stored on our database to display high scores to other players.
  • Device Identifiers: We share your Android Advertising ID with AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Approximate Location: We use IP addresses to determine your general location (City/Country) to provide ads in the correct language.
  • App Activity: We track game interactions and high scores to improve gameplay and maintain the leaderboard.
  • Performance Data: We collect Crash Logs and Diagnostics via Google Play to identify bugs and ensure stability.

2. Data Security and Encryption

We take your data security seriously. All data, including Leaderboard Nicknames, is encrypted in transit using secure protocols (HTTPS). This ensures your information remains protected while being processed.

3. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob. AdMob may use your information to personalize ads. You can opt-out via your device's "Google Settings > Ads" menu.

For more details, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Your local progress and "Mute Preferences" are stored on your device.
  • Server Storage: Your Username and High Scores are stored on a secure database to enable the Global Leaderboard. You can request the deletion of your leaderboard data by contacting us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

Neon Dash is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us.
