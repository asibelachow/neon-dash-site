Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2026

Welcome to Neon Dash. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information and data security when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect and Share

Neon Dash does not require any account registration or personal login. However, we use Google AdMob and standard analytics to function. The following data is collected and shared with third-party service providers for advertising and performance monitoring:

  • Device Identifiers: We share your Android Advertising ID with AdMob to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Approximate Location: We use IP addresses to determine your general location (City/Country) to provide ads in the correct language.
  • App Activity: We track game interactions (like high scores and button clicks) to improve gameplay and monitor ad performance.
  • Performance Data: We collect Crash Logs and Diagnostics to identify bugs and ensure the game runs smoothly on all devices.

2. Data Security and Encryption

We take your data security seriously. All data collected and shared with third-party partners is encrypted in transit using secure protocols (HTTPS). This ensures that your technical identifiers remain protected while being processed.

3. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob. AdMob may use your information to personalize the ads you see. You can opt-out of personalized advertising at any time through your Android device's "Google Settings > Ads" menu.

For more details, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

4. Local Storage

Your game progress, such as your "Best Score" and "Mute Preferences," is stored directly on your device using Local Storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers and can be cleared by deleting the app's cache or uninstalling the game.

5. Children's Privacy

Neon Dash is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.
